# Code of Conduct

We want brig·id to be a respectful, safe, and constructive space for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Focus discussions on ideas and implementation, not people.
- Help keep the organization welcoming to new contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated disruptive or bad-faith behavior

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security report or contact the maintainers through the brig·id organization.
